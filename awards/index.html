awards

2024
AnitaB.org Advancing Inclusion Scholarship The AnitaB.org Advancing Inclusion Scholarship supports women and nonbinary technologists by helping them access AnitaB.org's career-advancing programs, including Grace Hopper Celebration. Grace Hopper Celebration is AnitaB.org's flagship conference and is described by the organization as the world's largest gathering focused on women in tech, offering networking, community, and professional development opportunities.
2019
Computing Research Association‑Women (CRA‑W) Scholarship CRA-WP, the Computing Research Association's committee focused on women in computing research, runs scholarships and award programs designed to support women at important stages of their computing journey. Its programs emphasize research exposure, mentoring, recognition, and professional development for students pursuing computing-related academic and research careers.
2016
Grace Hopper Student Scholarship The Grace Hopper Student Scholarship helps students attend the Grace Hopper Celebration in India, where they can take part in technical sessions, career development, mentoring, and large-scale networking with peers, researchers, and employers. AnitaB.org presents these scholarships as a way to make participation in Grace Hopper Celebration more accessible to students and professionals in tech.
IET India Scholarship (Runner Up) The IET India Scholarship Award recognizes high-performing undergraduate engineering students in India and evaluates not just academic performance but also applied thinking, creativity, leadership, and engineering problem-solving. The program is open to students from AICTE- or UGC-approved institutions who meet the required academic criteria, and runner-up recognition reflects strong national or regional performance in a competitive engineering scholarship program.
National Network for Mathematical & Computational Biology Research Fellowship The National Network for Mathematical and Computational Biology fellowship is tied to an India-based research network created to promote research and training in mathematical and computational biology. Its goal is to build interdisciplinary capacity by connecting students and researchers with projects and mentoring in areas at the intersection of mathematics, computation, and biology.
2015
Indian Academy of Sciences Research Fellowship The Indian Academy of Sciences Research Fellowship, commonly offered through the Science Academies' Summer Research Fellowship Programme, enables students and teachers in India to spend about two months working with scientists affiliated with the national science academies on short-term research projects. The fellowship is meant to provide hands-on research exposure, mentorship, and deeper engagement with academic science.
