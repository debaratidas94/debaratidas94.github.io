# About Me
I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Minnesota NLP group led by [Prof. Dongyeop Kang](https://dykang.github.io/) at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I am also co-supervised by [Prof. Jaideep Srivastava](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Y4J5SOwAAAAJ&hl=en) who leads the Data Mining and Research Group (DMR) at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I also work as a research assistant to [Prof. Jisu Huh](https://cla.umn.edu/about/directory/profile/jhuh) who heads the Minnesota Computational Advertising Laboratory ([MCAL](https://mcal.umn.edu/))

Broadly, my research aims to combine NLP with computational social science.
Specifically, I am interested in understanding human behavior through NLP and graph learning methods.
 
I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, and my Bachelor's degree in Computer science from PES University, Bangalore, India.
